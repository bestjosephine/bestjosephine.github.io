---
title: "Think Bigger -- A Conversation with My Former Leader"
date: 2026-05-15
tag: "diary"
---

Recently, I communicated with my former leader. Last month, he was promoted to a vacant but higher position, which means he left our team.
To be honest, I was surprised when he said let's have a talk. In my opinion, if he were not my boss anymore, we wouldn't have any connection at all because we are in two different teams. I didn't understand his behavior or thoughts.
But we had a nice and deep talk last night. He showed me what he was doing in his new team. And when he talked about his work, his eyes were shining, his voice went up, and his smile grew big. I was caught up in his excitement.
I felt jealous. I don't have a destination and I don't love my job. But I saw destination and dream and love for work in him.
After showing me what he was doing, he suddenly asked me a question: "If you were the head of your product (an intelligent chatbot to provide customer service), how would you design it?" I was too nervous and astonished to say a word. Finally, I said haltingly, "A good question…" And then he explained his thoughts on the product.
I asked him why he had so many ideas. In my head, I couldn't find any. I didn't have any thoughts on my AI chatbot. He answered from two aspects. Firstly, know the technology. Secondly, think. Think about the value behind the product, the department, the company, and even the whole Internet.
Finally, he encouraged me to think bigger and dream bigger. Think, what a simple and great word! And what a hard word! I am still confused. I have to admit, although I didn't understand what he had done in the last year, he really taught me a lot yesterday.
And now, I think I should talk more with other people. I will start with my friends.
